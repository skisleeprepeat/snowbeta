Welcome to Snowbeta.

This is a prototype website that provides various visualizations of data from the USDA-NRCS SNOTEL system. It is implemented with the Python Flask framework and Plotly.

SNOTEL is a hugely important service comprised of around 900 active remote mountain weather stations provided by our government. Snowpack data provides certainty and planning to farmers and cities for the entire western water supply and river runoff each year. It also serves on one of the most important long term datasets to understand and track climate change indicators in the western United States. These indicators include declining annual snowpacks, forward shifts in the timing of snowmelt and peak snowpack accumulation dates, fewer days below freezing, more precipitation falling as rain rather than snow, and greater variability/unpredictability in storm sizes and annual snowfall.

If you want to learn more about those issues, some good places to start are here and here.
